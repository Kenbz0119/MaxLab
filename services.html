Red Light Therapy

Harness the power of light to heal from the inside out.

Red Light Therapy (RLT) uses specific wavelengths of red (630–700nm) and near-infrared (700–1100nm) light to penetrate deep into skin and tissue. This stimulates the mitochondria — your cells' energy factories — triggering a cascade of natural repair processes that reduce inflammation, accelerate healing, and rejuvenate tissue at the cellular level.

At MaxLab, our medical-grade panels deliver precise, calibrated doses so every session is maximally effective and perfectly safe for all skin types.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy

Flood your tissues with healing oxygen under pressure.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) involves breathing 100% pure oxygen inside a pressurized chamber — typically at 1.5 to 3 times normal atmospheric pressure. This drives oxygen deep into plasma, cerebrospinal fluid, and tissues that normal breathing can't reach, dramatically accelerating cellular repair.

HBOT is trusted by professional athletes, post-surgery recovery specialists, and neurological rehabilitation programs worldwide. At MaxLab, our mild-pressure chambers offer a comfortable, highly effective experience for everyone from weekend warriors to those recovering from chronic illness.

What Happens in a Session

1
Pre-Session ScreeningQuick health check to confirm safe pressure settings for your session.
2
Pressurization & Oxygen DeliveryRelax inside the chamber as pressure gradually rises and 100% oxygen flows.
3
Gradual DecompressionPressure is slowly and safely released while you rest and rehydrate.
