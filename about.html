Meet Sadie

Regenerative Wellness Specialist · St. George, UT

Sadie's journey into regenerative wellness began not in a clinic, but in a personal search for answers. After witnessing the profound difference that advanced bioscience therapies made in her own recovery, she dedicated herself to bringing those same transformative results to others.

With years of specialized training in photobiomodulation, ozone therapies, and hyperbaric oxygen protocols, Sadie founded MaxLab Regenerative Wellness with a single mission: to make cutting-edge healing accessible, personalized, and deeply effective.

She believes that the body has an extraordinary capacity to heal when given the right tools — and she's built MaxLab as that place where science and compassion work hand in hand.
